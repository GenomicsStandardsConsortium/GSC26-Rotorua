# Visit Rotorua

Rotorua is a small regional city renowned for its rich Māori culture. It provides access to some of Aotearoa New Zealand’s most iconic natural attractions and experiences: geothermal wonders, beautiful lakes, native forests with hundreds of kilometres of mountain biking trails, and natural hot springs and wellness experiences.

For more information to help plan your time in Rotorua see: [Visit Rotorua NZ](https://www.rotoruanz.com/visit)
