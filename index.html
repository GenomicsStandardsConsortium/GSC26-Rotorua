GSC26 Rotorua, NZ.

The 21st international meeting of the Genomic Standards Consortium will held in co-located with the [ISME meeting](https://isme-microbes.org/isme-symposia/isme20/) in New Zealand. The GSC26 meeting will take 12-14th August (place the week prior to ISME) in Rotorua, approx 230KM south of Auckland.
