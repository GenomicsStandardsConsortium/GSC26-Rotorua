GSC26 Rotorua, NZ.

The 26th international meeting of the Genomic Standards Consortium will be held the week before the 2026 ISME meeting in New Zealand. The GSC26 meeting will take 12-14th August in Rotorua, approx 230KM south of the ISME venue in Auckland.
